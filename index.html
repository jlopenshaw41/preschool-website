About

We're a friendly and welcoming preschool, situated at the heart of Littleton village, in the beautiful Ribble Valley.

We provide a nurturing environment for children aged 2 to 5, where little ones are given the space and support to thrive

Dedicated outdoor play areas allow children to explore and flourish, whilst building a lifelong love of learning.

Our aim is to provide the best start for our youngest children as they begin their journey.
